Allies of the Republic of America

Kingdom of Eldia

Headed by Empress Annie Reiss, the Kingdom of Eldia rules over much of mainland Europe. They also share a border with the New Russia Federation, and consistently holds good relations with the Republic of America.

The New Russian Federation

Headed by President AKuznetsov, the New Russian Federation rules over all Russian lands, and holds lands in the Balkans and western Europe. They have previously engaged with the Republic, but has since made amends.

The United Nations

Founded by the joint actions of the Republic of America, the New Russian Federation and the Kingdom of Eldia, the United Nations was formed as a peacekeeping initiative to prevent member states from declaring war on one another.
