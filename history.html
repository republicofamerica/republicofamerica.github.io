The Generation System

The Republic has been "restarted" several times, so much so that it is a common debate as to what "generation" the Republic is on. Whenever the Republic restarts, at the will of Disclarity or someone else, the generation progresses. November 2014 and the following months were the first generation, and the latest generation (presumably the 15th) started around November 21st 2019.

Before the Republic restarts, the Republic must first die. Although it does sometimes die from inactivity, a more common death (especially earlier in the generations) we're the result of full scale revolutions from within the Republic itself. Many of which accuse founder Disclarity of corruption or nepotism. Many prominent members of the Republic have been known to participate in these revolutions.

The Russian Mole

In the 6th Generation Dakota Kingsbury, then Controller of the Republic, gave Republic secrets to JohnathanFarrow of the Soviet Union in exchange for asylum. the information leaked included the existence of the Official Citizens Database, which was a program run by the Administrative Council where data was collected on every Republic citizen. The scope of the information is still classified to this day due to it's sensitive nature.

Although pardoned by President Virginia Jackson, Dakota Kingsbury still receives backlack from this event to this day, and has been asked multiple times to be held accountable for his actions.
